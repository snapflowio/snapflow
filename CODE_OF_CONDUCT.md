# Code of Conduct

We're all here because we care about building something great. Treat everyone the way you'd want to be treated.

## What we expect

- Be kind and respectful.
- Assume good intentions.
- Give honest feedback without making it personal.
- Take feedback without taking it personally.
- Don't harass, insult, or troll anyone.
- Don't share someone's private information without their permission.

## Reporting issues

If someone is making the community a bad experience for you, reach out to us at support@snapflow.io. We'll handle it fairly and keep it private.

Maintainers can remove content, reject contributions, or restrict access for anyone whose conduct harms the community.

## Scope

This applies anywhere you interact with the Snapflow community: issues, pull requests, discussions, chat, and events.
